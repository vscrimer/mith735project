We are digital humanists and performance studies scholars using AnyStyle in Ruby to extract and parse bibliographic references from academic journals.

We plan to run citation analysis on ten years of back issues, focusing on four Theatre and Performance Studies journals:

  • TDR
  • Theatre Survey
  • Theatre Journal
  • PAJ

    • We are currently in the process of cleaning up the initial data for all 2019 issues of TDR and Theatre Survey which is available on this site. We will continue to make new data available in the same location.
